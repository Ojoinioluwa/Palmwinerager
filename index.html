ABOUT PALMWINERAGER

PALMWINERAGER is not just an event, it is a cultural movement that seeks to weave the threads of tradition into fabrics of the modern day world. Inspired by the rich heritage of palmwine gatherings in various African cultures, this event reimagines and reinvents the traditional concepts, infusing it with contemporary elements to create a unique immersive experience.
